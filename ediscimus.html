Edendo discimus

Teaching with digital editions

Edendo discimus part of the editio maior initiative. The aim of this project is to integrate digital editions more and more into traditional teaching. This page lists the 307 text encodings of papyri produced so far in various workshops and regular papyrological courses by Holger Essler. May the overview serve as a nice reminder for the participants and as an incentive for other colleagues to become active in this direction themselves. The papyri encoded in classes taught by Federica Nicolardi are listed below. Holger Essler was responsible for the final editing of all texts.
