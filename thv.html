Seminars in Digital and Public Humanities

Seminars 2020/2021

In 2008, on the initiative of Gianluca Del Mastro, the Centro Internazionale per lo Studio dei Papiri Ercolanesi (CIPSE) began building a full-text database of Herculaneum papyri (Thesaurus Herculanensium Voluminum, THV) in collaboration with the Chair of Classical Philology I at the University of Würzburg (WÜ). Under the direction of Gianluca Del Mastro (CISPE) and Holger Essler (WÜ), a total of 26 texts were entered and made available at www.thvproject.it (this address has since been reassigned and contains other material). From 2013 Holger Essler (WÜ) coordinated the transfer of existing texts and the entry of new texts into the Digital Corpus of Literary Papyri (DCLP), which is now part of papyri.info. A maior contribution was made by Vincenzo Damiani during his employment for the Anagosis project (WÜ) directed by Essler. Today, THV is part of Essler's editio maior initiative and thus also benefitting from encodings during his teaching at Ca'Foscari University of Venice (VE). With 178 texts and 297 inventory numbers THV contains almost all Greek and most Latin papyri from Herculaneum in searchable form.
The texts were entered and revised on the basis of the latest complete edition. Later partial editions or new editions are listed in the bibliographical references.
The following list contains a complete overview of published texts from Herculaneum. Texts not yet digitized are marked with *.
